## Goal
Only parse `title:` / `status:` from inside the YAML frontmatter block so body text can't shadow them.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Parser starts after the first `---` line and stops at the closing `---`
- [ ] Tickets whose body contains a line beginning with `title:` or `status:` still report the frontmatter values
- [ ] Tickets without frontmatter are skipped or reported as unknown rather than crashing

## Notes
[report_generator.py:347-355](report_generator.py#L347-L355) scans the whole file and breaks once both fields are seen — body lines can poison the result. Consider keying on the `---` fence or switching to a small YAML loader once `pyyaml` is a dependency.
